Homemade Lasagna

Description

The lasagna recipe you see below is one of the most satiating meals for a family whether it be a warm summer night
or the dead of winter. This multi-layer masterpiece will be sure to make everyone happy.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Bacon
  • Grits
  • Sausage

Steps

  1. Get the slop hot.
  2. Mix the slop.
  3. Layer the slop.
  4. Bake the slop at 450.
  5. Perfect! You're slop is hot and the should have a crispy top.
